How It Works

WorldCupSwarm operates as an autonomous pipeline. When match highlights become available, the system discovers them from public sports news RSS feeds and YouTube search, verifies the content is genuine World Cup 2026 material, scores it for quality and relevance, and then edits it with branded overlays and original captions.

Users who authorise the application via TikTok's official OAuth flow grant permission for WorldCupSwarm to publish video content to their TikTok account on their behalf. The application only posts content that the account owner has pre-approved through the permission grant, and never accesses private data beyond what is required to publish.
