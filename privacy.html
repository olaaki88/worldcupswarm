Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WorldCupSwarm ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you authorise our application to publish content to your TikTok account.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorise WorldCupSwarm through TikTok's official OAuth flow, we receive:

  • Access Token: A time-limited token that allows us to publish video content to your TikTok account on your behalf.
  • TikTok User ID: A unique identifier associated with your TikTok account, used only to direct content to the correct account.

We do not receive or store your TikTok password, private messages, follower list, or any other personal profile data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the access token solely to:

  • Publish World Cup 2026 highlight video content to your TikTok account on your behalf.
  • Verify the status of video uploads submitted via the TikTok Content Posting API.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, analytics resale, or any purpose beyond the content publishing described above.

3. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens are stored in a secure, encrypted environment and are never exposed in logs, public repositories, or third-party services. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted storage of all credentials
  • Access restricted to authorised systems only
  • Tokens are rotated or revoked when no longer needed

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to any third party. Your TikTok access token and user ID are used exclusively within the WorldCupSwarm system and are never shared with advertisers, analytics providers, or other external services.

5. TikTok Platform Data

WorldCupSwarm complies with TikTok's Platform Terms of Service and Developer Policies. Any data received through the TikTok API is used solely for the purpose for which it was granted. We do not cache TikTok user data beyond what is operationally necessary to complete a publishing request.

6. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as the publishing service is active. You may revoke access at any time through TikTok's "Manage App Permissions" settings, which will immediately invalidate the token and prevent any further publishing.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your TikTok account at any time via TikTok's app settings
  • Request deletion of any stored data we hold about you
  • Contact us for information about what data we hold

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ola@the-connect.co.uk.

8. Children's Privacy

WorldCupSwarm is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
