Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WorldCupSwarm collects, uses, and protects information when you authorise our application to publish content to your TikTok account.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorise WorldCupSwarm through TikTok's official OAuth flow, we receive:

  • Access Token: A time-limited token that allows us to publish video content to your TikTok account on your behalf.
  • TikTok User ID: A unique identifier used only to direct content to the correct account.

We do not receive or store your TikTok password, private messages, follower list, or any other personal profile data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the access token solely to publish World Cup 2026 highlight video content to your TikTok account and verify the status of video uploads.

3. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens are stored in a secure, encrypted environment and are never exposed in logs, public repositories, or third-party services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to any third party.

5. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as the publishing service is active. You may revoke access at any time through TikTok's "Manage App Permissions" settings.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke WorldCupSwarm's access at any time via TikTok Settings → Privacy → Manage App Permissions. To request data deletion, contact us at ola@the-connect.co.uk.
